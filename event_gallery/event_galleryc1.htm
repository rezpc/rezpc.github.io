Street Image 11th Anniversary - Seguin, TX (NOVEMBER 9)

Street Image was proud to celebrate its 11th year Anniversary since the day it was founded.  The gathering was held at the Silver Center located in central Seguin, TX, starting at mid afternoon and going on strong up until the 12:00am closing time.  The after-party soon followed as it directed itself to a local nightclub, then off to the President's house for a final wrap-up.

DJ Entertainment provided by 2 Heartbeats from New Braunfels, and security provided by Seguin's Finest.

Here are some pics from the event, we hope you enjoy a peek into a Street Image party, and wish that next year you can come join us!
