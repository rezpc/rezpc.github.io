More Information
Cherry-Black paint with patterns and ribbons, covered with gold and red metal flakes.  The tracker rolls on gold-centered knock-offs, held on with a chrome bullet cap, wrapped in 205/50/R15 tires. 

The interior is covered in maroon and grey diamond-tucked crushed velvet that I upholstered myself.  A 2-channel 2540 Logic mounted in the rear of the vehicle push 640 watts to two 12" Hollywood Daddy Excursions.

At the 1st Annual Chicano Style Car Show I took 1st place in the category of Semi-Custom truck, 1st place for Best Paint, 1st place for Best Truck Engine, and 1st place for Best Interior.  I've also won 2nd place at the Lowtimes Car Show in Houston, TX under the category of Street Custom Mini S.U.V..

In the future I plan to install a 1-pump CCE setup with 2 dumps and 3 12v batteries, as well as installing some maroon neon lights on the undercarriage.

—Jeremy Bustos
